Since its inception in 2013, the Ezbet Project has had a profound impact on the local community of Ezbet Abu Qarn. With our work there now complete, it's time to shift our focus and expand our reach. Thus, we are transitioning the Ezbet Project into Kairos City Lab.
Kairos City Lab, often referred to simply as Kairos Lab, will now serve a broader community base. We remain steadfast in our commitment to training, research, and assisting local communities in need, striving to write a new chapter of our story.

Kairos City Lab is proudly sponsored by The Academy of Scientific Research and Technology. Our offices are situated within the Faculty of Engineering at Ain Shams University.

Our new website is currently under construction here at www.kairoscitylab.com.
